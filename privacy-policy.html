Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

Introduction

Marcopass ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data: We collect GPS location data to detect which countries you visit. This is the core functionality of our app that allows us to award you digital passport stamps.

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and profile information you choose to provide.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including countries discovered, badges earned, and interactions with features.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Provide and improve our services
• Detect country visits and award stamps
• Enable social features like leaderboards and friend connections
• Send you relevant notifications (with your permission)
• Analyze app performance and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services provided by Google. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

• Firebase (Authentication, Database, Storage)
• Google Places API (Location services)
• BigDataCloud API (Country detection)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access your personal data
• Request deletion of your account and data
• Opt-out of promotional communications
• Disable location services (note: this will limit app functionality)

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
